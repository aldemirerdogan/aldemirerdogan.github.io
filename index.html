About me

I have been working as a assistant proffesor at Batman University, Department of Electronics and Communications.

My research interests lie primarily in the area of digital image processing and image analysis, data coding, computer and Network security and cryptography.

Apart from the professional life, I enjoy spending my spare time being in nature and taking photos. I spend a large amount of my free time exploring the latest technology advancements in the field of technical computing and electronics Engineering. Reading fictions books and science and technology books are among the things I like doing most.
